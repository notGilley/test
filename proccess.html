Process of Completion

Step 1: Initial Contact

The customer reaches out to us to begin the process of building their custom PC.

Step 2: Gathering Build Details

We contact the customer to gather detailed information about their desired build specifications.

We will also ask for a budget to help narrow down the options.

During this stage a parts list will be paid and confer with customer about finalizing the price.

Step 3: Invoice Generation

We generate an invoice based on the agreed-upon build details and send it to the customer.

Step 4: Payment

The customer pays the invoice to proceed with the order.

Step 5: Ordering Parts

We order the necessary parts for the build.

*At the moment, There is no inventory, so we order on demand until we can build and inventory*

Step 6: Notification of Parts Arrival

Once the parts are delivered, we notify the customer.

Step 7: Building the PC

We begin assembling the custom PC.

Step 8: Testing

After assembly, we run benchmarks and stress tests to ensure the PC performs optimally.

Step 9: Delivery

We coordinate with the customer to arrange a time and place for delivery of the completed PC.
